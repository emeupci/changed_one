EME Region

Europe & the Middle East


The Europe and Middle East Region spans across the EurAsian Continent; from picturesque streets of Lisbon in the west to the breathtaking wilderness of Kamchatka in Russia's far east. The divirsity of landscapes is eclipsed only by the diversity of people groups. There is one unifying truth; everyone, everywhere has need of a Savior. This the mission of the Europe and Middle East United Pentecostal Church: to share the good news of a powerful Savior.

Capturing the hearts of mankind and Cities for Christ.
